ARKNIGHTS

Through The darkness, we see light
take on the role of a key member of Rhodes Island, a pharmaceutical company that fights both a deadly infection and the unrest it leaves in its wake.
Together with your leader Amiya, you'll recruit Operators, train them, then assign them to various operations to protect the innocent and resist those who would thrust the world into turmoil.
