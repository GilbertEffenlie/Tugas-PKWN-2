GAME INFO

Arknights (Chinese: 明日方舟; pinyin: Míngrì Fāngzhōu, "Tomorrow's Ark") is a free-to-play tactical RPG/tower defense mobile game developed by Chinese developer Hypergryph.
It was released in China on 1 May 2019, in other countries on 16 January 2020 and in Taiwan on 29 June 2020.
Arknights is available on Android and iOS platforms and features gacha game mechanics.

An anime television series adaptation by Yostar Pictures has been announced. A spin-off game, Arknights: Endfield, was announced for PC and mobile devices in March 2022.
